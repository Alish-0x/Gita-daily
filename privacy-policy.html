Introduction

Gita Daily ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We collect minimal personal information:

  • App Usage Data: We track which verses you've viewed and saved to provide streak functionality
  • User Preferences: Language preference, theme settings, reminder time, text size preferences
  • Progress Data: Your current day in the 108-day journey, streak count, best streak

2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Analytics Data: App opens, feature usage, crash reports (via PostHog)
  • Ad Data: Ad impressions and clicks (via Google AdMob)

3. What We DO NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or files
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect payment information

How We Use Your Information

  1. To Provide Core Features
    • Track your reading progress and streaks
    • Save your favorite verses
    • Send daily reminder notifications
  2. To Improve the App
    • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
    • Fix bugs and crashes
    • Understand which features are most used
  3. To Show Advertisements
    • Display banner ads to support free app development
    • Ads are served by Google AdMob

Data Storage

  • All personal data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences
  • Analytics data is sent to PostHog for app improvement purposes
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Display advertisements
  • Data Collected: Device info, ad interactions
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

PostHog Analytics

  • Purpose: App analytics and crash reporting
  • Data Collected: App usage events, device info
  • Privacy Policy: https://posthog.com/privacy

Permissions Required

The app requests the following permissions:

Permission Purpose
INTERNET Load ads and send analytics
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Reschedule notifications after reboot
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM Schedule daily reminder notifications

Children's Privacy

Gita Daily is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Retention

  • Analytics data is retained for 12 months
  • You can request deletion of analytics data by contacting us
